Bunny Chow

Serving Size: 1 person

A bunny-chow is basically made from half a loaf of bread with the inside scoop out. The inside is kept and used to absorb the gravy while you eat the bunny-chow. The hollow part of the bread is filled with lots of delicious authentic Indian curry. The curry is either made with, Mutton, Beef, Chicken, Mince And even vegetables
