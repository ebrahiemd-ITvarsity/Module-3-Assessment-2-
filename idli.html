Idili

Serving Size: 1 person

Idle could be a soft & downline steamed cake manufactured from hard rice & lentil batter. These are one among the healthiest super molecule packed Breakfasts from South Indian cuisine. Idle is created with Brad dekaliter (injured black gram) and rice.
